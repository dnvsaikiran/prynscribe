Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

1. Information We Collect

PrynScribe collects your email address and basic profile information via Google OAuth to provide cross-device synchronization. We also collect audio data processed during your transcription sessions.

2. How We Use Information

We use your data to generate transcripts, summaries, and MCQs for your personal study use. Your audio is processed via secure AI APIs (Deepgram/Groq) and is not shared with third parties for marketing.

3. Data Storage

Transcripts and user preferences are stored in Firebase. You can delete your data at any time via the user dashboard.

4. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, contact us via our website.
